Hi RunNaters,

RunNat Day 2014 is here! We are very excited to share with you the vision for RunNat Day this year and how you can be part of it.

RunNat Day 2014 will be happening on the 4th of October and the theme this year is “Run. Pray. Believe. REBUILD THE WALLS”, inspired by Nehemiah 2:17.

RunNat Day Klang Valley will be seeing some major changes in format this year. Instead of being one large centralized run all around the different parts of the Klang Valley, we will be decentralizing the run into 4 zones, and all the zones will be having their own prayer run happening at the same time with their own Finish Line Celebrations. We want our runs to have more impact by being focused on communities.

We are also working out with the churches involved the possibility of a post run community project for each zone where runners and others get to “gotong royong” to bless a community. On top of that, runners will be given a chance to raise funds for an individual, family or a community that is part of our GRACE Project for RunNat Day 2014. Once you’ve registered as a runner and are part of a team, you can use your RunNat online page to choose a beneficiary and share on social media to start the fund raising process!

The distance for each checkpoint will be reduced to 3km, instead of the 5km in previous years. However, if you wish to run to more than one checkpoint, you are welcome to do so too! As part of our efforts to be more inclusive, we will also be introducing a WALK CATEGORY.

To take part REGISTER ONLINE at http://runforthenation.com/runnat2014/register.php OR click on the sign up button below Sign up by 16th September to avoid not getting your t shirt on time for the run.

See you at the Finish Line!
